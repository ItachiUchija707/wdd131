F1 Legends

I chose this name because it evokes a clear identity and association with Formula 1 which intends to attract people who is interested in the sport

Site Purpose

This website will provide access to F1 information such as races, drivers, results and circuits. A place where fans of the sport can track their teams, drivers and results in one place

  • Race circuits
  • Drivers
  • Race results including qualification sessions

Color Scheme

Primary Color: #e10600 (F1 Red) - Used for headers and primary buttons.

Secondary Color: #15151e (Carbon Dark) - Used for backgrounds and text contrast.

Scenarios

  • "Where can I find race results from the previous races?"
  • "Who are the drivers for this season?"
  • "What are the tracks for this season?"
  • "Who is leading the race"

Typography
